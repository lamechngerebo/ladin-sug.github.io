Meaning of SUG

The Student Union Government (SUG) is the highest representative body of students in tertiary institutions. It serves as the voice of students, protecting their rights and ensuring proper communication between students and school management.

The importance of SUG lies in its ability to maintain order, organize student welfare, coordinate academic and social activities, and ensure that students are fairly represented in all institutional matters.

SUG holds strong dominance within the student community as it regulates activities, resolves conflicts, and serves as the bridge between the student body and the institution’s authority.
