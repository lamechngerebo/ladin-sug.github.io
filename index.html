What is the SUG?

The Student Union Government (SUG) acts as the bridge between the student body and the college administration. It is a governing body that wields significant influence, ensuring that student welfare, academic standards, and extracurricular rights are protected. Through the SUG, students have a collective voice to negotiate, advocate, and lead, effectively holding dominance in student affairs to foster a conducive learning environment.
