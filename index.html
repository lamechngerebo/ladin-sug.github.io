About the SUG

The Student Union Government (SUG) is the recognized governing body for students. Its primary importance lies in its role as the bridge between the student body and the institution's administration. By ensuring student welfare, advocating for academic rights, and maintaining a structure of representation, the SUG exercises significant influence and dominance in student affairs, ensuring every student has a voice in the governance of the college.
