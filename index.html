The Dominance & Importance of the SUG

The Student Union Government (SUG) is the apex governing body representing the interests, rights, and welfare of the student populace. Its importance cannot be overstated; it serves as the critical bridge connecting the student body directly to the college administration.

Operating with recognized dominance in student affairs, the SUG commands the structural authority to advocate for academic fairness, organize social and sports development, and negotiate policies that directly impact the campus environment. As a student governing body, it ensures that the collective voice of the students is not just heard, but actively shapes the 2025/2026 academic experience.
